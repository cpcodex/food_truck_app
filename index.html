Welcome to the Blue Ridge Wrap Wagon!

Rolling with flavor, fresh from the mountains.

Discover one of the many great food trucks in our area!

From our family to yours, we're thrilled to share our passion for fresh, local food with the West Virginia community. As a husband and wife team, we craft delicious wraps that are the perfect on-the-go meal. Each one is made with a soft flour or whole wheat tortilla and packed with the freshest ingredients we can find, all sourced from local West Virginia farmers. It's our way of bringing a true taste of the Blue Ridge Mountains to hikers, travelers, and anyone who wants a quick, wholesome bite. We can't wait to serve you!

Menu

*Price includes a drink and one side of your choice

The Cheat River Chicken Wrap $12.99

Tender grilled chicken, crisp lettuce, juicy tomato, and shredded cheddar cheese, drizzled with a creamy ranch dressing. A fresh and satisfying classic.

The Appalachian Veggie Wrap $9.99

A colorful medley of roasted vegetables—including zucchini, bell peppers, and onions—with a zesty herb spread. A delicious option that celebrates the best of the local harvest.

The Seneca Steak & Cheese Wrap $13.99

Thinly sliced steak grilled with onions and bell peppers, topped with melted provolone cheese. It's a hearty and savory wrap that will fuel any adventure.

The Mountaineer BBQ Pork Wrap $13.99

Slow-smoked, locally sourced pulled pork smothered in a tangy BBQ sauce with a kick, and topped with our signature homestyle coleslaw. A true taste of the mountains.
