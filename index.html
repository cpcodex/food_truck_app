Discover one of the many great food trucks in our area!

From our family to yours, we're thrilled to share our passion for fresh, local food with the West Virginia community. As a husband and wife team, we craft delicious wraps that are the perfect on-the-go meal. Each one is made with a soft flour or whole wheat tortilla and packed with the freshest ingredients we can find, all sourced from local West Virginia farmers. It's our way of bringing a true taste of the Blue Ridge Mountains to hikers, travelers, and anyone who wants a quick, wholesome bite. We can't wait to serve you!
